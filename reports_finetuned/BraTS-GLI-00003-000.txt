IMPRESSION:
The imaging findings suggest a high-grade glioma involving the left frontal lobe. The mass shows intense ring enhancement on T1c, which is characteristic of glioblastoma multiforme (GBM). The surrounding edema on T2-FLAIR and T2w sequences further supports the diagnosis. Given the aggressive nature of the lesion, immediate consultation with a neuro-oncologist is recommended for further management options, including surgical resection and potential radiation therapy.
